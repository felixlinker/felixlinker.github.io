Coriolis

In July of 2018 I authored my first commit to the open source project coriolis. coriolis is an online service maintained by the players of the MMORPG-style space-sim Elite: Dangerous. It allows players to plan how they want to equip and modify their space-ships and give them detailed statistics. It’s a plain front-end JavaScript project that is written with React.

In October of 2018 the team of coriolis decided that the code-base needed a major re-work. This is why I started ed-forge which is a TypeScript library that handles all the logic of coriolis and is meant to serve the community of Elite: Dangerous as a backbone for any services that deal with loadouts of space-ships.
